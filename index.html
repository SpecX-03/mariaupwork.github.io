The 7 key tips to help you get the best auto insurance for less:

1) Get Multiple Insurance Quotes & Save

One of the best ways to save your money and find cheap car insurance online is by requesting various quotes from multiple insurance companies. Car Insurance Advisors has many partners which can help find you cheap auto insurance quotes.

2) The Car You Drive Matters

The type of car you drive impacts the premium you pay. The model and year would impact your final coverage price. For instance, if you are planning to buy insurance online for a brand-new BMW or Ferrari, it would be much more expensive than insuring a Honda Civic.

3) Find The Right Deductible

To get car insurance online, consider increasing your deductibles. Most major insurance companies will offer a lower monthly premium with higher deductibles. Though they would cost more in case of an accident they could save you money in the long term if you are an accident free driver.

4) Grouping Your Insurance Policies

Consider bundling your auto and home insurance policies to lower your premiums. Most major insurance companies offer a discount for bundling your policy, some offer up to a 25% discount. If you have other insurance policies more discounts might be available.

5) Keep Your Credit Score Secure

Maintaining a high credit score could, in some states and instances,lower your auto insurance costs. In one study, applicants with excellent credit score saved up to 54% on auto insurance compared to those with lower credit scores.

6) Always Claim Available Discounts

Make sure you claim all available discounts to help lower your car insurance. Many discounts are available through professional associations, member and employee benefit, union memberships, etc. Some online car insurance companies offer a discount for safe drivers using an electronic device. If you own more than one car, make sure you ask about multi-vehicle discount. There are many discounts available, be sure to inquire about them to help you find cheap car insurance online.

7) Avoid Coverage Overages

Not all coverages are created equally. To save and find inexpensive car insurance online, make sure you are selecting the coverage you need. First and foremost, you should find out what is your state’s minimum legal requirement and then compare additional coverages. Do you drive an older car? Maybe you do need full coverage as the replacement cost of your vehicle might be very low. Are you a light driver? Ask the insurance company if they have lower monthly costs for low mileage drivers. Shopping and comparing coverages could save you hundreds of dollars on your car insurance policy.

Different companies have different ways to calculate your rate and they differ in the discounts available, therefore it is advised you compare rates to see if you can find lower insurance rates.

A great way to save on your insurance premium is to combine your insurance policies. If you already have home insurance policy take advantage of bundle discounts offered by most insurance companies. Make sure you insure all your cars with the same company to qualify for the multi-vehicle discount.
